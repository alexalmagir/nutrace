# Market Research Summary

> Curated public summary derived from the internal founder venture brief and competitive analysis.

---

## Market size and urgency

- ~40% of the global population experiences functional GI disorders (Rome Foundation, multiple epidemiological studies)
- IBS prevalence nearly doubled post-COVID: 6.1% → 11%
- 76% of IBS patients still report symptom management as difficult (AGA 2024)
- Patients lose 3.6 work days/month to IBS symptoms (up from 2.1 in 2015)
- Food-related triggers are a top suspected cause but rarely systematically tracked

## Buyer and user landscape

| Role | Relationship to Nutrace |
|---|---|
| Patient (primary user) | Captures meals + symptoms, reads patterns, exports PDF |
| Gastroenterologist | Receives PDF, uses it as structured input during consultations |
| Registered dietitian | Same as above; may drive retention via "bring your log next session" |
| Payer / insurer | Out of scope for MVP; DiGA (Germany) flagged as Phase 3 option |

## Why now

1. Vision and voice AI are cheap and accurate enough to remove diary friction (GPT-4V, Whisper)
2. Clinician-facing structured PDF is a 2026-era format that fits existing workflows (no portal adoption required)
3. Post-COVID GI symptom surge created durable patient demand for diagnostic support

## Competitive landscape

| Competitor | What they do | Gap Nutrace fills |
|---|---|---|
| Cara Care | Manual food and symptom diary, coaching add-on | Manual entry is the abandonment driver |
| mySymptoms | Manual food diary with correlation detection | Same — no frictionless capture |
| Bowelle | Manual IBS tracker | Same |
| MyFitnessPal et al. | Nutrition/calorie tracking | Not positioned for symptom correlation |

**Nutrace's wedge:** photograph-in, voice-in, proactive trigger alert at capture time, and a PDF that fits into a 15-minute consultation. No manual forms, no macro tracking, no wellness framing.

## Risks flagged in the venture brief

- Regulatory creep if product language sounds diagnostic → locked down by governance
- Vision AI accuracy on mixed dishes → mitigated by 2-tap correction flow
- Retention if first insight takes too long → target: ≤3 days to first pattern
